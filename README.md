The DJI Neo 2 Fly More Combo is a compact, lightweight drone designed for creators who want powerful and intelligent aerial filming capabilities in a portable package. It stands out with features like omnidirectional obstacle sensing, gesture control, 4K video capabilities, and versatile flight modes that make it beginner-friendly yet exciting for experienced pilots.

## Design and Portability  
Weighing just 151 grams, the Neo 2 is incredibly light and easy to carry, making it ideal for travel and outdoor activities such as running, hiking, biking, and family outings. Its compact size allows for effortless palm takeoff and landing, and it includes full-coverage propeller guards for safe indoor flying and around people.

## Camera and Video Performance  
The Neo 2 sports a 1/2-inch CMOS 12MP camera capable of recording 4K video at 60fps, with slow-motion options up to 4K/100fps and 2.7K vertical video suited for social media reels and shorts. Stabilization is provided by a 2-axis gimbal combined with electronic image stabilization (EIS), ensuring smooth footage during flight and tracking.

## Flight Features and Safety  
One of the hallmark features is the omnidirectional obstacle sensing using multiple sensors around the drone, significantly enhancing flight safety. It also offers gesture control for intuitive flying and camera operation, plus voice commands, app control, and compatibility with remote controllers and FPV goggles. ActiveTrack enables 8-directional following with fast and smooth tracking up to 12 m/s, perfect for capturing moving subjects.

Flight time can reach up to 19 minutes with a single battery, and the Fly More Combo pack includes additional batteries, a two-way charging hub, and spare accessories that extend flight sessions without interruption.

## Control and Connectivity  
The drone can be controlled via multiple means including traditional RC controllers, a smartphone app, gesture controls, voice commands, and even DJI FPV goggles for immersive flying experiences. The Fly More Combo enhances versatility by providing extra batteries and charging options, ensuring longer and more flexible flights.

## Who Should Buy the DJI Neo 2 Fly More Combo?  
- Aspiring drone pilots and beginners wanting a safe, easy-to-fly drone with smart obstacle avoidance.  
- Content creators who want high-quality, stable 4K footage without bulky gear.  
- Outdoor enthusiasts and families who need a portable drone for capturing active moments.  
- Travelers looking for a lightweight, compact drone with multipurpose controls.
